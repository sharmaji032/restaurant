Restaurant Policies

Effective Date: [Date]

1. Reservation Policy

We encourage our guests to make reservations in advance to ensure your preferred time and date. Reservations can be made via our website or by calling us directly.

1.1 Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel or modify your reservation, please do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation fee.

2. Dining Conduct

We aim to create a welcoming atmosphere for all guests. We ask that all patrons be respectful of staff and other diners. Any behavior that disrupts the dining experience may result in a request to leave the restaurant.

3. Dress Code

We have a smart casual dress code. Please avoid wearing beachwear, flip-flops, or overly casual attire.

4. Children and Minors

Children are welcome in our restaurant. However, parents are responsible for their children’s behavior and safety. We offer a children's menu for our younger guests.

5. Alcohol Policy

We serve alcoholic beverages to guests who are 21 years and older. Valid identification is required for all guests ordering alcohol.

6. Food Allergies

If you or someone in your party has food allergies or dietary restrictions, please inform our staff when placing your order. We take food allergies seriously and will do our best to accommodate your needs.

7. Payment Policy

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payment options. A gratuity of 18% may be added for parties of 6 or more.

8. Changes to Policies

We reserve the right to modify our policies at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website with the date of the latest revision.
