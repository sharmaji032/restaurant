Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

When you interact with our website or restaurant, we may collect personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Payment information (credit card details, billing address)
  • Reservation and order details
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide, such as dietary preferences or food allergies

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Time and date of visit
  • Referring website

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal and non-personal information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Our Services: To process your reservations, orders, and any requests you make via our website or at the restaurant.
  • To Improve Our Services: To better understand your preferences and improve the customer experience at our restaurant.
  • Marketing and Promotions: To send you promotional offers, updates, or newsletters (with your consent). You can opt-out of these communications at any time.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with legal requirements, such as tax and regulatory obligations.
  • Customer Support: To address your questions, concerns, or feedback.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience on our website. Cookies allow us to:

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Save your preferences for future visits.
  • Track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

4. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and will not share or sell your personal information to third parties, except under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, reservation platforms) to facilitate our services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal proceedings (e.g., subpoenas, court orders).
  • Business Transfers: If we undergo a business transaction such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our website uses SSL encryption to safeguard your data during transmission.

However, please be aware that no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal or business obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [Restaurant Email Address].

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., delivery partners, reservation platforms). These websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices. We encourage you to read their policies before providing any personal information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with the date of the latest revision. By continuing to use our website or services, you agree to the updated Privacy Policy.
